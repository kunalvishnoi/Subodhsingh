About Us

SHREE BANKEY BIHARI SUBODH SINGH CHARITABLE TRUST is a NGO which aims to spread and further the good work done by Martyr Inspector Subodh Kumar Singh. We work for social harmony and tend to bring about a difference in the lives of underprivileged families and communities.

Social work for us is nothing other than an opportunity which we can avail to serve our nation, our NGO tends to share the exact values. We address the basic but neglected issues of the poor and values the traditional wisdom of the local people. We promote self-reliance, with prime attention to women and children and also works towards development of the urban and rural poor population.

We are government registered and verified. Shree Bankey Bihari Subodh Singh Charitable Trust as a citizen group raises awareness and influence people, independent cooperatives, community associations, socities, groups and various associations.

We provide and fulfils certain services, development-oriented tasks and works with aims and objectives to bring about required positive changes in society, community, areas and situations. What good is an NGO if it doesn’t fight for upliftment and betterment of weaker section of community to bring them in the mainstream of society and move the society towards more improved and developed way of living.

Our vision stands for :-

संघे शक्ति कलौ युगे।

संघे शक्ति कलौ युगे।

UNITY IS STRENGTH. We work on this ancient Sanskrit phrase to compel not just our members but the whole of community to come together and work in harmony.
